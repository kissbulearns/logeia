Logeia

Record. Remember. Retrieve.

A private second brain for iPhone. Save anything you read, watch, or listen to — Logeia summarizes it with on-device AI and brings it back the moment you need it. Your library never leaves your phone.

How it works

📥 Record Save articles, X posts, and YouTube videos from any app via the share sheet — or add URLs, notes, screenshots (with text recognition), and pasted text directly. Saving is instant and works offline.
🧠 Remember Apple Intelligence summarizes and files every item on your device — short summaries, key takeaways, and topics. Listen to any article or transcript with on-device speech, star favorites, and track what you've read.
🔍 Retrieve Instant full-text search, find-in-page like a PDF reader, jump-to-the-moment search inside video transcripts, and "Ask your library" — grounded answers from what you've saved.
Private by architecture, not by promise.
Logeia has no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. All AI runs on your device. We couldn't read your library even if we wanted to — the app has nowhere to send it. Read the full privacy policy →

Availability

Logeia is in development and heading to TestFlight. It requires an iPhone with Apple Intelligence (iPhone 15 Pro or newer, iOS 26). Watch this page or the GitHub repository for the TestFlight link.
